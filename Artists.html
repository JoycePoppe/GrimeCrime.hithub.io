Artists

Grime Crime is a rappers collective consisting of 4 grime artists. They all have a criminal past that they still express in their music. Nowadays they are on the right track (please be told), but that does not mean that they want to forget their criminal past. It has had an important impact on their lives, and certainly on their music. They started Grime Crime to respond to the system with music instead of violence / crime as before.

BATCHY

Batchy is a 26-year-old grime artist from Hackney, North London, UK. He has a heavy past, but is now on the right track. He loves grime music and lives in it.

BIGMAN BIZZLE

Bigman bizzle is a 25yo grime artist from south london, uk. He is coming to Antwerp, Lotto arena to perform as a headliner at our highly anticipated LABEL NIGHT.

LADY SCENT

Lady Scent, still a minority in the grime scene but that is what makes her unique. With her 23 years of age and with here self-confidence she is coming to Belgium with her squad to perform for the fans.

DUST

Dust, an emerging grime artist from Blackpool carrying a troubled and criminal past, drags you along in his fight for equality/equal opportunities. His songs are loaded with an intense temper, which is simply magnetizing.
